Job Title: Design Engineer

Job Description:
We are looking for a creative and experienced Design Engineer to join our team! As a Design Engineer, you will be responsible for researching and developing new ideas for designing our new and existing products.

Duties and Responsibilities:
• Drafting blueprints and engineering plans
• Designing and developing new products
• Developing design strategies
• Enhancing existing products
• Performing tolerance analysis of new products and changes to existing products
• Assisting in the development of product specifications
• Overseeing the manufacturing process
• Maintaining product design documentation
• Collaborating with design and product teams
• Identifying ways to improve product efficiency

Requirements and Qualifications:
• Minimum of a Bachelor’s Degree in Design Engineering or Product Design
• Minimum of X years of experience as a Design Engineer or similar role
• Strong math skills
• Proficiency in MS Office
• Proficiency with design software
• Excellent verbal and written communication skills
• Highly developed attention to detail
• Strong time management skills
• Strong analytical skills
• Excellent problem-solving skills
• Ability to work well in a team environment
• Ability to work in a fast-paced environment
